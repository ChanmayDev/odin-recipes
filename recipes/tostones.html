Fried Plantain Chips

These will be salty and crunchy.

They will go great with some guacamole.

Ingredients

  • Plantain
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Heat up pan with your choice of oil. (I like to use coconut oil).
  2. Peel plantain.
  3. Cut into half inch wide pieces.
  4. Place pieces in pan and fry for one minute on each side to soften.
  5. Place on a cutting board and smash them a little with a flat surface.
  6. Once flatten place back in the pan and fry until golden brown on each side.
  7. Salt to taste!

    8. Enjoy!
